ttyl

Stop texting and driving.

About

Designed to stop texting and driving, ttyl will automatically send responses to anyone who contacts you while you're using google maps to navigate. The automatic response will inform the person that you're driving, and include your estimated time of arrival.

How It Works

To use this application, simply indicate when you start your trip. If "driving" is activated, the app will automatically respond to your contacts and let them know you can't respond currently. It will look like this:

"Your name" is currently driving! They will respond to your message when they arrive at their destination.
